Rose

The rose, a timeless symbol, holds diverse meanings across cultures and contexts. Primarily associated with love and beauty, the red rose signifies deep affection and passion. Meanwhile, white roses embody purity and innocence. Yellow roses convey friendship and joy, while pink ones express gratitude and admiration. Beyond love, roses can symbolize balance and mystery, with their thorns representing life's complexities.
