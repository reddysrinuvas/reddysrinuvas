Creepers

Creepers typically refer to crawling or climbing plants that grow along the ground or on other structures, using their stems, tendrils, or roots for support. These plants often exhibit a creeping or trailing growth habit, spreading horizontally rather than growing upright. In a broader context, "creepers" can also be used metaphorically to describe people or things that move slowly and stealthily, often with a sense of suspicion or unease. In popular culture, the term gained recognition through the iconic hostile mobs known as "Creepers" in the video game Minecraft. These pixelated creatures silently approach players and explode, causing damage to the player and the surrounding environment. The term has since been colloquially adopted to describe anything or anyone that gives off a similar sense of gradual, unpredictable threat or discomfort.
